### Confirm directly with the speaker

- Meisam Razaviyayn: the proposal says *From Theory to Throughput: Unifying Architectures and Scaling Deep Memory via Online Optimization*. A public ICLR workshop listing uses *From Theory to Throughput: Unifying Architectures and Scaling Deep Memory* without the final phrase.
- Jiawei Zhang: *Optimization for Efficient Training and Inference* appears to be a workshop-specific title; no independent public listing was located.
- Kaiqing Zhang: the proposal says *Towards Understanding and Post-training LLMs as Decision-Making Agents: A Regret-based Approach*. His current ICML 2026 paper is titled *Post-Training LLMs as Better Decision-Making Agents: A Regret-Minimization Approach*.
- Andrey Kharitenko: the proposal says *Constrained Optimization through Denoising and Applications to Data-Driven Control*. His current ICLR 2026 paper/talk is titled *Landing with the Score: Riemannian Optimization through Denoising*.

These differences do not necessarily indicate errors: invited speakers often use workshop-specific titles. They should not be silently changed without direct confirmation.
